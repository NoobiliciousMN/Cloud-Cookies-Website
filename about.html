Founded in 2024, Cloud Cookies was born from a simple idea: to create the softest, most irresistible cookies that bring a little joy to every bite. We’re passionate about crafting cookies that are not only delicious but feel like a treat for the soul—soft, gooey, and packed with flavor. Every cookie is made with love and care, ensuring each bite is as heavenly as the last. At Cloud Cookies, we believe in making life’s sweet moments even sweeter. Whether it’s a personal treat, a gift for a loved one, or a celebration, we’re here to deliver happiness, one soft cookie at a time.

Vision

Our company aims to become Malaysia's leading soft cookie provider through innovative flavors, premium quality ingredients, and a commitment to maintaining our signature soft texture while setting nationwide standards

Mission

We aim to deliver Malaysia's finest soft cookies through diverse channels while building trust-based relationships and maintaining exceptional quality standards in every delicious bite we create.
